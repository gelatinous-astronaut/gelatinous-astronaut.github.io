My Digital Art

I took some graphic design classes in high school and have been making digital art ever since then! In particular, I'm really interested in three main modes of creation:

Collage - using found and lost media dredged from internet archives, I explore the emergent properties that are formed from the interactions between divergent media and themes. The combination of modern digital processing with media from a bygone era creates new forms and new expressions.

Research & Data - data visualization in its most basic form, or highly processed images derived from data used for research. send me your numbers! you can find some animated visualizations on my Space Simulations Research page

Generative - a form that I intend to explore more of, simple rules can generate new images that are interesting in and of themselves, or can be used as ingredients for further processing
